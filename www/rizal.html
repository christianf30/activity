Rizal Monument


The Rizal Monument is a memorial in Rizal Park in Manila, Philippines built to commemorate the executed Filipino nationalist, José Rizal. Address: 1225 Roxas Blvd, Ermita, Manila, Metro Manila, Philippines Height: 42′ 0″ Opened: December 30, 1913 Material: Granite Completion date: 1913 Designer: Richard Kissling Did you know: The biggest monument of Rizal in the world is in Calamba, Laguna.
