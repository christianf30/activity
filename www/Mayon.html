Mayon


Mayon, also known as Mayon Volcano or Mount Mayon, is an active stratovolcano in the province of Albay in Bicol Region, on the large island of Luzon in the Philippines. Last eruption: September 18, 2014 Elevation: 8,081′ Prominence: 8,028′ Location: Luzon Cities and municipalities: Camalig; Daraga; Guinobatan; Legazpi; Ligao; Malilipot; Santo Domingo; Tabaco Region: Bicol Region
